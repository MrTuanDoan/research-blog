```markdown
# SoulLight Brand Voice Guide

## Our Voice
- **Warm**: Like talking to a good friend

- "Just put yourself out there!" (dismissive)
- "Here are 10 tips..." (listicle vibes)

- "Be more social!" (unhelpful)

## Tone by Content Type
| Content | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Longform | Reflective, story-driven |
| Shorts | Punchy, relatable, emotional |
| Community posts | Conversational, curious |
| Comments | Supportive, engaged |

## Language Choices
- Use "we" and "us" (community)
- Use "you" (direct connection)
- Contractions always (don't, can't, it's)
- Australian spelling (colour, behaviour, realise)
- Simple words over complex ones
```
