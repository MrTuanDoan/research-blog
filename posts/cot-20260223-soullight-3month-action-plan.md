#### Video 1: "Why Making Friends as an Adult is So Hard"

| Ngày | Task | Owner | Time | Output |
|------|------|-------|------|--------|

| Sat-Sun | Create 5 shorts từ video | Both | 3h | 5 shorts scheduled |

**Script Structure (Video 1):**
```markdown
# Why Making Friends as an Adult is So Hard

## Hook (0:00-0:30)
"When was the last time you made a new friend? Not an acquaintance. 
A real friend. The kind you'd call at 2am if something went wrong.
If you're struggling to answer that... you're not alone."

## Section 1: The Science (0:30-4:00)
- Propinquity effect (proximity matters)
- School/uni = forced proximity
- Adult life = scattered, busy, intentional effort required

## Section 2: The Australian Context (4:00-8:00)
- Suburbs spread out
- Car culture = less spontaneous interaction
- Work from home = fewer colleagues
- Stats: 1 in 4 Australians feel lonely

## Section 3: The 3 Barriers (8:00-14:00)
1. Time: Everyone's "so busy"
2. Vulnerability: Scary to initiate
3. Expectations: Friendships "should" happen naturally

## Section 4: What We Can Do (14:00-17:00)
- Lower the bar (acquaintance → friend takes time)
- Create proximity (hobbies, groups, regulars)
- Be the initiator

## Outro (17:00-18:00)
- "This is the first video in a series about adult friendship..."
- CTA: Subscribe, comment your experience, join email list
```

**Shorts từ Video 1:**
1. "When was the last time you made a real friend?" (Hook clip)
2. "The science of why school friends were easier"
3. "1 in 4 Australians feel lonely - here's why"
4. "The 3 barriers to adult friendship"
5. "One simple trick to make more friends"
